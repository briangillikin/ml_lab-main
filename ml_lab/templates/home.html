About the 4138 form:

The 4138 is used by a veteran (or others with firsthand information) to provide context regarding a veteran's VA benefits request

About the 4138 classifier:

This classifier is used to help route veteran benefit requests to the apppropriate VA work queue.

Classifications include: BVA_APPEALS_REMAND, COMPETENCY_ISSUE, CUE, DEPENDENCY_REQUEST, DEPENDENCY_REQUEST_REMOVAL, PAYMENT_ISSUE, DMC_ISSUE, EXAM_ISSUE, FOIA, MEDICAID_NURSING_HOME, NOTICE_OF_DEATH and UNKNOWN
